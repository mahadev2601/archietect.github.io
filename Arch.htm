About the Studio

Architecture rooted in
human experience

Founded in 2004 by Elena Hartmann and Marco Voss, FORMA is an internationally recognized studio at the intersection of architecture, art, and urban design. We believe that great buildings are more than structures — they are living narratives that evolve with the people who inhabit them.

Our multidisciplinary team of 80 architects, designers, and strategists works across scales — from intimate private residences to landmark public institutions — always guided by the same principle: that thoughtful design has the power to transform how we live.

Elena Hartmann
Founding Principal & Creative Director

How We Work

Our design process

01

Discovery & Vision

Deep listening sessions to understand your life, aspirations, and the unique potential of your site. We begin every project by asking the questions that matter most.

02

Concept Design

Translating insights into architectural concepts. We develop multiple spatial narratives, testing ideas through models, sketches, and immersive visualizations.

03

Design Development

Refining the approved concept into a fully resolved design — coordinating structure, systems, and materials to create a seamlessly integrated whole.

04

Realization

Meticulous construction administration, with our team on-site ensuring that every detail of the design is executed to the highest possible standard.
